Puebla Facts

Puebla, in full Puebla de Zaragoza, city, capital of Puebla estado (state), central Mexico. It deserves to be on your Mexico touristic destinations to visit list, This city is situated about two hours away from Mexico City. It’s known for its culinary history, colonial architecture and pottery.

It is the capital and largest city of the state of Puebla, and the fourth largest city in Mexico, after Mexico City, Monterrey, and Guadalajara. A colonial era planned city, it is located in the southern part of Central Mexico on the main route between Mexico City and Mexico's main Atlantic port, Veracruz—about 100 km (62 mi) east southeast of Mexico City and about 220 km (140 mi) west of Veracruz.
